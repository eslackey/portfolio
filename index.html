my projects

recent work

Seamless Service Station

This was a proof of concept to replace standard price scanners in stores with an iPad. The iPad enables self service features such as search, weekly ad, store maps, and more. I created initial prototypes and the visual design that went to production. The proof of concept has been expanded to a 70+ store pilot.

myDevice Single App Concept

Store PDA devices had over 60 task related applications. I designed a consolidated app concept that combined all of the most frequent tasks for team members into a single iPod touch app. The concept was the foundation of a large initiative which rolled out iPod touch devices to the entire chain.

Team Member Recognition

This proof of concept allowed team members in stores to give recognition to each other using an iPad. The iPad was docked in the break room and replaced paper recognition cards that are frequently exchanged in stores. I designed the user interface of the iPad application that was tested in stores.

Mobile LOD

The Leader on Duty (LOD) is in charge of nearly everything that happens in a store. This proof of concept provided all of the data an LOD needs, right at their finger tips. I designed early prototypes and iterated the design to ensure we provided all the most critical data at a glance.

Gamification

Gamification was a proof of concept that tested the motivational impact of rewarding team members with points, badges, and digital recognition for their work. I designed game concepts for many store tasks, user interface concepts, and a library of badges for the test.

Ship from Store Dashboard

I consulted on a project that needed to create an easily consumable, mobile dashboard to track shipments from stores. The process was originally ran from an excel spreadsheet. I converted the spreadsheet data into a simple, visually appealing dashboard.

Compare and Attach

This application concept was created to give team members access to better sales tools. It gives the ability to compare products and recommend attachment items. I designed the user interface for the concept and it has been approved as future chain-wide enhancement on team member devices.

Point of Sale

One of the most important components of retail is the checkout experience. I had the opportunity to redesign Point of Sale for Target stores. I faced constraints of legacy hardware, performance requirements, and numerous edge-case scenarios. I worked closely with the point of sale experts to create the new design concept.

Emergency Flipchart Mobile

Every now and then an emergency happens in a store. Previously, there were numerous packets, checklists, and guides for emergencies. This app consolidates all of the in-store emergency procedures into a single application. It also syncs emergency phone numbers for all devices in-store. I created the concept design and initial prototype.
